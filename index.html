[ SYNOPSIS ]

The Story

Ajinkyan follows the journey of Ajinkya, a young cadet at Ajinkyadwar Sainik School, Satara — a prestigious military residential school nestled in Maharashtra. Bright, passionate, and full of dreams, Ajinkya enlists with hopes of becoming a decorated soldier and making his family proud.

But life inside the school is far from what he imagined. Beneath the discipline and camaraderie lies a world of complex hierarchies, unspoken rules, and emotional battles. As Ajinkya navigates friendships, rivalries, and the weight of expectation, he begins to question what it truly means to serve — not just a nation, but oneself.

Ajinkyan is a deeply emotional coming-of-age story that explores identity, sacrifice, belonging, and the invisible wars fought within — long before any battlefield is ever reached.

"Some legacies aren't inherited. They're lived."

— Ajinkyan, 2027
